Affidavit of Lawful Money Demand
Annual Declaration Pursuant to Federal Statute
12 U.S.C. § 411 • 28 U.S.C. § 1746
being competent to testify and of lawful age, do hereby make the following declaration under penalty of perjury pursuant to 28 U.S.C. § 1746:
Declaration
1. I have, throughout the calendar year ____________, consistently and without exception demanded that all funds deposited into my accounts be redeemed in lawful money of the United States pursuant to 12 U.S.C. § 411, which provides: "They shall be redeemed in lawful money on demand at the Treasury Department of the United States."
2. I have maintained a contemporaneous Lawful Money Redemption Ledger documenting every deposit, the instrument type, the amount, and confirmation that each was endorsed with the restrictive endorsement demanding lawful money.
3. The endorsement language used on all negotiable instruments deposited by me during this period reads substantially as follows:
"Deposited For Credit On Account.
Redeemed In Lawful Money Per 12 U.S.C. § 411
by: [Name], grantor, all rights reserved"
4. For electronic deposits where physical endorsement was not possible, I provided written notice to my financial institution(s) that all incoming funds are to be received as lawful money. A copy of said notice(s) is maintained in my records.
5. The total amount redeemed in lawful money during the above-stated period is: $ ________________
6. I have retained copies of all endorsed instruments, bank statements, and correspondence related to this demand. These records are maintained as contemporaneous business records within the meaning of the Federal Rules of Evidence, Rule 803(6).
7. At no time during this period did I voluntarily accept Federal Reserve Notes as private credit. All funds received were demanded and redeemed as lawful money under the authority of Congress as codified at 12 U.S.C. § 411.
8. I make this affidavit for the purpose of memorializing my consistent exercise of my right to demand lawful money, and for any and all lawful purposes to which it may serve.
I declare under penalty of perjury under the laws of the United States of America that the foregoing is true and correct. Executed on this _______ day of __________________, 20_____.

28 U.S.C. § 1746: "Wherever any matter is required or permitted to be supported by a sworn declaration, the declarant may, in lieu of an oath, submit an unsworn declaration subscribed as true under penalty of perjury."
