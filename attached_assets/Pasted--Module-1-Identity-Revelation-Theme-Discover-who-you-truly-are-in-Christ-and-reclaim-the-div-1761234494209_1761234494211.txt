🩸 Module 1 — Identity & Revelation

Theme: Discover who you truly are in Christ and reclaim the divine identity Babylon tried to name over you.

Learning Outcomes:

Understand the difference between legal identity and spiritual identity

Learn how names, numbers, and registrations became instruments of Babylonian control

Receive revelation of your divine sonship and royal priesthood

Recognize how covenant restores the original image of God in man

Core Teachings:

The Father’s Image and the Son’s Redemption — Genesis 1:26–27, Romans 8:14–17

Name and Authority — “To them gave He power to become the sons of God.” (John 1:12)

The False Identity System — How birth certificates, licenses, and SSNs create “persons” under state jurisdiction

Rebirth and Renaming — Revelation 2:17, the white stone name known only to Him and you

Practical Expression:

Draft your Declaration of True Identity — a personal covenant statement identifying yourself as a Child of God and Citizen of Heaven.

Replace legal self-concept with divine self-awareness: begin signing correspondences “In Faithful Stewardship, [Your Name], Trustee of [Trust Name].”

Activation & Reflection:

“Ask the Father to show you the name He calls you in heaven. Write it down. Let that name guide every act of stewardship.”

⚖️ Module 2 — Trust & Jurisdiction

Theme: Learn the structure of covenant trust as heaven’s legal system—the divine counterpart to Babylon’s commercial law.

Learning Outcomes:

Understand covenant trust as a living relationship, not a paper entity

Grasp the threefold structure: Grantor, Trustee, Beneficiary

Learn how to express heavenly jurisdiction on earth

Discern how Babylon’s “law of contracts” differs from divine “law of covenant”

Core Teachings:

The Heavenly Court: God as Grantor, Christ as Trustee, You as Beneficiary

The Separation of Title and Benefit: Why the Trust is Supreme

Jurisdictional Shifts: From Territorial to Ecclesiastical

Covenant Law vs Contract Law — “Swear not at all… but let your yea be yea.” (Matthew 5:34–37)

Practical Expression:

Create your Covenant Trust Charter (template provided in the Covenant Repository).

Learn to hold property “in trust” for Kingdom purposes.

Replace signatures “under penalty of perjury” with trust endorsements “in good faith and honor.”

Activation & Reflection:

“Hold a symbolic act of transfer—declare aloud that everything you have belongs to the Father’s trust. Release ownership; receive stewardship.”

💎 Module 3 — Stewardship & Legacy

Theme: Learn how to administer the Father’s resources with honor—protecting inheritance, multiplying provision, and establishing generational succession.

Learning Outcomes:

Understand stewardship as worship — faithful management of God’s property

Learn practical trust administration: property, finances, inheritance

Understand how to prevent probate and secure generational continuity

Learn how generosity and tithes function in covenant economy

Core Teachings:

The Kingdom Economy: From Scarcity to Stewardship — Matthew 25:14–30

Inheritance Through Covenant Succession — Numbers 27:8–11

Treasury Principles: The Ecclesiastical Economy

Charitable Trusts as Acts of Worship — 2 Corinthians 9:8

Practical Expression:

Draft a Trustee Ledger — your stewardship record between you and God (property, income, ministry works).

Establish your Covenant Treasury Account — a dedicated fund or wallet representing resources held in trust for Kingdom use.

Create a Legacy Letter to your heirs—spiritual and natural—explaining their inheritance as trust beneficiaries, not mere recipients.

Activation & Reflection:

“Ask God to reveal how He wants His resources to flow through you. Record your first act of stewardship as a covenant trustee.”

🕊️ Module 4 — Ambassadorship & Governance

Theme: Walk as an ambassador of Christ—governing from heaven’s authority, not Babylon’s permission.

Learning Outcomes:

Understand the rights, immunities, and responsibilities of an ambassador of heaven

Learn to operate under divine authority in public and private dealings

Understand how collective trusteeship expresses ecclesiastical governance

Learn how to establish local embassies or Ecclesia Houses as extensions of the Kingdom

Core Teachings:

Diplomatic Authority: We Are Ambassadors for Christ — 2 Corinthians 5:20

The Law of Representation: The King’s Seal and Your Word

Ecclesiastical Governance: The Body as a Living Administration

The Embassy System: Representing Heaven on Earth

Practical Expression:

Obtain your Ecclesiastical Trust ID as visual representation of ambassadorship.

Establish or join a local Embassy Circle—a gathering of trustees operating in unity.

Practice covenant adjudication—resolving disputes through scriptural process rather than Babylonian courts.

Activation & Reflection:

“Lay your hand over your heart and decree: ‘I am an ambassador of Christ, entrusted with the ministry of reconciliation. I govern in love, not by force.’”
