Create a modern, responsive website titled **“The New Covenant Trust”** that teaches freedom and sovereignty under the Nation/Body of Christ.  

Core Mission:  
- Show that Christ is the cornerstone and grantor of the New Covenant Trust.  
- Provide resources for people to reclaim their true identity in Him.  
- Expose counterfeit systems (Babylon, personhood, debt slavery).  

Structure:  

1. **Homepage:**  
   - Bold headline: "Freedom Under the New Covenant Trust."  
   - Sub-headline: "Reclaim your inheritance in the Body of Christ, outside Babylon’s counterfeit system."  
   - Call-to-action buttons:  
     - “Enter the Trust” (leads to overview page)  
     - “Begin Learning” (leads to education hub)  
   - Symbolic imagery: covenant scrolls, light breaking chains, Kingdom imagery (crown/tree of life).  

2. **Navigation:**  
   - Home  
   - About the Trust  
   - Educational Hub  
   - Videos & Teachings  
   - Freedom Resources  
   - Nation of Christ  
   - Contact  

3. **About the Trust Page:**  
   - Explain the New Covenant Trust: Christ as Grantor, us as beneficiaries.  
   - Contrast with Babylon’s system of contracts and merchandise.  
   - Key scripture anchors (Hebrews 8, Galatians 5, John 8:36).  

4. **Educational Hub:**  
   - Articles, guides, and downloadable PDFs.  
   - Topics:  
     - “What is a Trust?”  
     - “The Legal Fiction vs. The Living Man”  
     - “Freedom under Christ’s Law”  
     - “Exposing Babylon’s Counterfeit.”  

5. **Videos & Teachings:**  
   - Embedded library of short teachings.  
   - Categories: Covenant, Law & Freedom, Babylon Exposed.  

6. **Freedom Resources:**  
   - Practical steps to walk in covenantal freedom.  
   - Templates, prayers, lawful notices, study guides.  
   - Links to your ebook(s) and handouts.  

7. **Nation of Christ Page:**  
   - Teach the idea of Christ’s body as a real nation/kingdom.  
   - Emphasize citizenship in Heaven as primary.  
   - Covenant-based community vision.  

8. **Contact Page:**  
   - Secure form for questions or mentorship requests.  

Style Guide:  
- Colors: Deep midnight blue (truth/revelation), gold (kingdom/authority), and white (purity/light).  
- Fonts: Elegant serif for headings (covenantal, authoritative), modern sans-serif for body (clarity).  
- Aesthetic: Minimalist, symbolic, reverent yet powerful.  

Footer:  
- Copyright © [Year] The New Covenant Trust  
- Scripture reference (e.g. Hebrews 8:6)  
- Newsletter signup for updates.  

Make the site fully responsive, mobile-friendly, and optimized for speed.
