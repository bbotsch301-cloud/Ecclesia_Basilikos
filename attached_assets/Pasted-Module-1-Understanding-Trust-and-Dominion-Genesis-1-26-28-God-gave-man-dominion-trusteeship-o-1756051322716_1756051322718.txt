Module 1 – Understanding Trust and Dominion

Genesis 1:26–28 – God gave man dominion (trusteeship) over creation.

The Fall (Genesis 3) – breach of trust; dominion forfeited.

Romans 5:12 – through one man’s disobedience, sin and death entered.

Christ’s Restoration – Jesus, the Second Adam, re-establishes trust (1 Cor. 15:21–22).

Module 2 – The Public vs. Private Identity

The Problem of Dual Identity

Public identity = citizen / legal person (fiction).

Private identity = living man/woman in God’s image.

A transmitting utility is required to bridge these realms in commerce.

Spiritual Parallel

Man lowering status → operating as a “person” under worldly law.

Christ raising status → making us heirs and sons of God (Gal. 4:7).

Key Truth: In Christ, believers have status correction:

100% man (earthly body) + 100% divine (seated in heaven, Eph. 2:6).

Module 3 – The Role of the Holy Spirit

The Bridge/Transmitting Utility

Just as a transmitting utility lets a man engage in commerce through a persona,

The Holy Spirit bridges heaven and earth, allowing believers to operate in both realms.

Scriptural Basis

Ephesians 1:13–14 – sealed with the Holy Spirit as the earnest (down payment) of our inheritance.

2 Corinthians 1:22 – Spirit as God’s seal and pledge.

Philippians 3:20 – citizenship in heaven.

Legal-Spiritual Analogy

Letter of credit in commerce = Spirit as a down payment guaranteeing future inheritance.

Suretyship of Jesus (Hebrews 7:22) ensures all liabilities are covered.

Module 4 – Documents of Identity in the Trust

Trust Declaration & Articles of Establishment

Establishes purpose, divine jurisdiction, and Christ as Grantor/Surety.

Beneficiary Authorization & Debt Discharge Agreement

Grants “the person” capacity to transact in the public, while affirming debts discharged in Christ.

Ecclesiastical Heirship Claim

Declares believer’s heirship to the Kingdom’s resources.

Notice of Divine Jurisdiction and Authority

Public affirmation that one operates under Kingdom law, not secular jurisdiction.

Spiritual Currency Ledger (Faith-Based Transactions)

Tracks faith transactions as fulfilled in Christ (Philippians 4:19).

Debt Discharge Notices / Statements of Faith

Used in prayer, covenant declarations, and trust operation: “Paid in full by Christ.” (John 19:30).

Module 5 – Operating “In Christ”

2 Corinthians 5:20 – Ambassadors for Christ.

John 17:21–23 – One with the Father, as Jesus is one.

Practical Application:

Living man = agent on earth.

Redeemed man = principal in heaven.

Liability rests with Jesus, the Surety; believer acts with authority but without liability.

Module 6 – Contrast: Babylonian System vs. Kingdom System

Babylon / Federal Reserve

Operates on debt and fiat trust (“full faith and credit” of the U.S.).

Currency = debt-based, unstable, perpetually dependent.

Kingdom of Heaven Trust

Operates on faith and divine promises.

Spiritual currency = faith-based promissory notes backed by God’s Word (Heb. 11:1; Phil. 4:19).

Surety = Jesus Christ, securing all obligations permanently.

Module 7 – Status Correction: Living as Sons

Galatians 4:7 – “You are no longer a slave but a son; and since you are a son, God has made you an heir.”

Romans 8:17 – co-heirs with Christ.

Living as Heaven’s Agents on Earth

Operate with ambassadorial authority.

Discharge debts spiritually and legally in Christ.

Walk as sons, not slaves to worldly systems.

✅ Summary Statement for Believers:
“In Christ, I am both a living man on earth and a redeemed son seated in heaven. The Holy Spirit is my seal, my transmitting utility, and my divine letter of credit. Jesus is my Surety, guaranteeing all obligations paid. As an ambassador of the Kingdom, I operate with full heavenly authority, free from worldly bondage, and entrusted to steward God’s estate.”
